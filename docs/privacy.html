1. Who We Are

SIC Ventures LLC (“SIC Ventures,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is the developer and operator of Harbor, a personal finance clarity app available on iOS and Android. Harbor is designed to help individuals in their 20s through 40s understand their complete financial picture by calculating and displaying their net worth across asset and liability categories.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Harbor mobile application (the “App”). By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information (Optional). You may create an account using your email address or phone number. Account creation is entirely optional — you may use the App as a guest without providing any personal information. If you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address or phone number (used for authentication via one-time passcode)
  • Financial input data you voluntarily enter: asset categories, liability categories, estimated values, and optional labels

Guest Mode. If you use the App without creating an account, all data you enter is stored locally on your device only. No information is transmitted to our servers in guest mode. If you later create an account, you will be given the option to merge your local guest data into your account.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Authentication Data. When you request a one-time passcode, we temporarily store your email address or phone number alongside the passcode for up to 10 minutes for the sole purpose of verifying your identity. This record is deleted immediately after successful verification or expiration.

Device and Technical Data. We may collect limited technical information necessary to operate the App, including device type, operating system version, and app version. This information is used solely for debugging and improving app stability.

Analytics Data (Planned, Not Yet Active). We intend to integrate privacy-respecting analytics in a future release to understand how users interact with the App. When implemented, we will update this policy and provide opt-out mechanisms where required by law. No analytics tools are active in the current version of the App.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what Harbor does not collect in its current version:

  • Social Security numbers, government-issued ID numbers, or tax information
  • Payment card or bank account numbers
  • Data from your device contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Data from financial institutions (planned for a future Plaid integration — see Section 9)
  • Data shared with advertisers or advertising SDKs
  • Browser cookies or browser-based tracking data (Harbor is a native mobile app)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity via one-time passcode and issue a secure session token
  • To store and display your financial input data and net worth calculations within the App
  • To maintain historical snapshots of your net worth over time so you can track changes
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App’s functionality and reliability
  • To respond to your support inquiries or privacy requests
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your financial input data for any purpose other than providing Harbor’s core functionality to you. We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, credit assessment, or sale to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers. We use a small number of trusted third-party service providers to operate the App. These providers access your data only as necessary to perform services on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose. Current service providers include:

  • Resend (transactional email delivery for one-time passcodes)
  • Railway (cloud infrastructure and database hosting for the Harbor API and PostgreSQL database)

Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, or governmental request, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of SIC Ventures, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers. In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice via prominent in-app notification or email prior to any such transfer.

With Your Explicit Consent. We will not share your information with any other third party without your explicit, informed consent.

5. Data Storage and Security

Encryption in Transit. All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Our API is served exclusively over HTTPS in production.

Encryption at Rest. Your data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway, which provides encryption at rest. Session tokens are stored on your device using encrypted secure storage (expo-secure-store).

Access Controls. Our production database is not publicly accessible. All API requests require a valid JWT session token. Founder access to production infrastructure requires multi-factor authentication.

Authentication. Harbor uses one-time passcode (OTP) authentication via email or phone. We do not store passwords. OTP codes expire after 10 minutes and are deleted upon use or replacement.

Limitations. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we implement commercially reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law, including the Pennsylvania Breach of Personal Information Notification Act (PANSIA).

6. Data Retention

Account and Financial Data. Retained for the duration of your active account. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of your request, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Authentication Records. OTP codes and associated email/phone records are deleted within 10 minutes of issuance (upon use, replacement, or expiration). Session tokens expire after 30 days.

Guest Mode Data. Stored locally on your device only. Deleted when you uninstall the App or clear app data. SIC Ventures has no access to or copy of guest mode data.

Technical Logs. Server logs may be retained for up to 90 days for debugging and security purposes, after which they are deleted.

9. Planned Third-Party Financial Account Integration (Plaid)

The current version of Harbor does not connect to any financial institutions. All financial data is entered manually by users.

A future version of Harbor is planned to offer optional account linking via Plaid Inc. (“Plaid”), which would allow users to automatically retrieve account balances for the purpose of calculating net worth. This feature will not be enabled until all applicable security, legal, and compliance requirements are satisfied.

When Plaid integration is activated, the following will apply:

  • Account connection will be entirely optional. Manual data entry will always remain available.
  • Harbor will retrieve account balance data only — not transaction history, credit scores, income, or any data beyond what is necessary to calculate net worth.
  • Before any Plaid connection is initiated, users will be shown explicit consent language and must affirmatively authorize the connection.
  • Plaid access tokens will be encrypted at the application layer using AES-256 before being stored.
  • Users may disconnect their linked accounts at any time. Upon disconnection, Harbor will revoke the associated Plaid access token and cease retrieving data.
  • Plaid’s own End User Privacy Policy governs Plaid’s data practices and is available at: https://plaid.com/legal/#end-user-privacy-policy

This policy will be updated with full Plaid disclosure language before that feature is made available to users.
